@Loxodromes are spiral lines that go around the earth, from pole to pole around the Earth, keeping a constant angle.
The red line is a straight meridian from the North Pole

If α= 0±180° → the line is a meridian
If α= 90±90° → the line is a parallel

Parallels and meridians are both loxodromes

The distance between London and Rio is a loxodrome, and the angle between the line and the meridian = α
Loxodromes ≠ @geodetic_line because they are not the shortest distance between 2 points
@Orthodromes = geodetic lines which are shorter
