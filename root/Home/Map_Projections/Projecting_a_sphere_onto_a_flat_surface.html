Distortions

There are 3 things that can change when a 3D surface is mapped onto a 2D surface

  • Lengths
  • Angles
  • Area


Conformal projections preserve the angles
Equal area projections preserve area
A projection can be conformal or equal area but not both

There is no projection that can preserve all lengths. Not to be confused with @true_scale mapping. True scale maps are those where some lines (but not all) are the same on the map as on the globe).
