Created Friday 27 May 2022

@GEOGRAPHY @YEAR3 @WATER_AND_ATMOSPHERE

Reasons why @discharge is important:

  • Prediction of floods/ drought
  • Need to know how much water can be extracted
  • Flow velocity → aquatic ecology
  • Infrastructure design depends on water pressure (wells, weirs, sluces etc.)

Storativity

@Storativity is a measure of storage.
A storativity of 0.12 means that 0.12m or water will be stored if the groundwater level rises by one metre.

Incidental Measurements


Measurements can be @incidental_measurements (measure once then leave) or @continuous_measurements (ongoing measurements at a set interval)
